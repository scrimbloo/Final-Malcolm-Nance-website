Malcolm Nance



Malcolm Wrightson Nance, an American author, media analyst, and special executive for counterintelligence, terrorism, retribution, and extortion, was born on September 20, 1961. He is a retired Senior Chief Petty Officer in the United States Navy who specializes in naval cryptology.



Also being an author, Malcolm Nance is best known for his books on counter terrorism and intelligence: An end to al-Qaeda, The plot to hack America, and Terrorist Recognition Handbook.
